About Me

I'm Lugmaan Noor, a Multimedia Specialist with over eight years of industry experience. My career has evolved from creative freelance roots to delivering high-standard work for private firms and government institutes, including Fenaka Corporation and the President's Office PR for the Viluntheri Raajje 2045 project.

I have built my reputation in photography and videography, covering high-profile meetings and events to produce cinematic content for both national TV and social media. This foundation fuels my expertise in graphic design and content creation, where I craft strategic social media identities and full brand assets. Additionally, my background includes 3D modeling, with a specific focus on interior architecture and furniture design.
